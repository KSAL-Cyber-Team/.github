# KSAL Cyber Team
We’re a small group of people from Kosovo and Albania who enjoy learning and practicing cybersecurity through Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges. This space is mainly used to share tools, writeups, experiments, and anything we build while learning.

We're not a formal cybersecurity team, just people who like breaking things, figuring out how they work, and learning together.

# What we do
Most of the time you'll find us working on things like:
- Web security challenges
- Cryptography puzzles
- Reverse engineering binaries
- Binary exploitation (pwn)
- Forensics challenges
- OSINT tasks
- Random CTF problems

# What you will find here
This organization is where we keep things like:
- Our own applications
- CTF writeups
- practice challenges
- tools and scripts
- random experiments
- learning resources

Everything shared here is for educational and ethical purposes only.
