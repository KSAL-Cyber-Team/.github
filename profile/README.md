# KSAL Cyber Team
We’re a small collective of cybersecurity enthusiasts from Kosovo 🇽🇰 and Albania 🇦🇱 who enjoy learning and practicing security through Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges.

Our group consists of students, hobbyists, and aspiring security professionals who like exploring different areas of cybersecurity by solving hands-on challenges and participating in competitions. While we’re not a formal organization, we collaborate to learn, experiment, and improve our skills together.

## What We Do
We mainly focus on CTF competitions and security practice, including areas such as:
- Reverse Engineering
- Web Exploitation
- Cryptography
- Digital Forensics
- Binary Exploitation (Pwn)
- Miscellaneous challenges

Through these challenges we develop practical skills, share knowledge with each other, and document what we learn along the way.

## What You'll Find Here
This GitHub organization is used to share things we build or learn during our CTF journey, such as:
- Tools and scripts created for CTF challenges
- Writeups and solutions
- Small security-related utilities
- Practice resources and experiments
- Collaborative learning projects
